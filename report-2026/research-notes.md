# Live Shopping 2026 — Research Notes (sourced facts)

US market only. All figures gathered June 2026 via web research; cite in report.

## Market size & adoption (US)
- US livestream e-commerce sales grew ~50% in 2025 to ~$14.6B (one widely-cited figure).
  Other market-research houses give larger "live commerce" totals (definitions vary: $32B 2023 -> ~$68B 2026; some cite ~$50B 2025). Use the ~$14.6B livestream-commerce figure as the conservative headline and note definitional spread.
- Livestream commerce ~5% of US e-commerce vs ~60% in China — large headroom.
- Adoption: ~12% of US shoppers have bought via livestream; another ~12% plan to try. ~60% of adults have watched a live shopping show, ~33% have purchased.
- Live shopping conversion rates often 9-30% vs 2-3% typical e-commerce.
- Social platforms led the market with ~42% revenue share in 2025.

## Whatnot (the standout)
- 2025 GMV: >$6B (some sources: $8B live GMV globally); revenue approaching $1B.
- GMV trajectory: 2020 ~$0.1B; 2023 ~$1.5B; 2024 ~$3B; 2025 ~$6-8B (roughly 2x YoY).
- Series F (Oct 2025): raised $225M at $11.5B valuation (doubled from start of 2025).
  ~$968M raised since 2019. Investors: DST Global, CapitalG (lead), Sequoia, a16z, Greycroft, BOND, Alkeon.
- Engagement: ~80-95 min/day per active user; >80% month-over-month retention; new buyers +285% YoY.
- #1 Shopping app in US and UK in 2025; 144 days in App Store top 20.
- Black Friday 2025: >$75-100M in live sales in a single day (~3x prior year).
- 140+ categories across 9 countries; ~20M new accounts added in 2025.
- Fastest-growing categories by GMV (2025): Beauty +791%, Electronics +444%, Jewelry +259%, Women's Fashion +223%. Women's fashion now fastest-growing segment.
- ~60% share of an estimated $22B North America + Europe live-shopping market.

## TikTok Shop
- US GMV: 2024 ~$9B (+650% YoY, 16 months post-launch); 2025 ~$15.1-15.8B (+~68% YoY).
- Global GMV 2025 ~$64B (+~94% YoY).
- Livestream share of US TikTok Shop GMV rose from ~10% (2024) to ~14% (2025); pre-recorded creator video still ~2/3 of sales.
- US sellers: ~4,450 (mid-2023) -> ~475,000 (mid-2025), ~5,000% growth.
- Even QVC gained 100k+ new customers via TikTok Shop in one quarter of 2025.

## Emerging / new platforms (US)
- Fanatics Live — trading cards & collectibles, card breaks (launched 2023).
- eBay Live — launched US 2022 (cards/collectibles); 2025 expanded to luxury watches, handbags, fashion; expanding to Germany & Canada 2026.
- StockX Live — announced; launching Summer 2026; sneakers, apparel, trading cards, collectibles, vintage fashion.
- NTWRK — limited-edition drops, streetwear, sneakers, collectibles, celebrity collabs.
- TalkShopLive — host-driven shoppable shows; NBCUniversal partnership (Aug 2024).
- Popshop Live — acquired by CommentSold (combined annualized GMV reportedly >$1.2B).
- Palmstreet — plants/collectibles live shopping (named among funded consumer marketplaces).
- Amazon Live, YouTube Shopping, Instagram/Facebook (Meta exited native LS 2022-23).

## QVC / HSN (home-shopping convergence)
- Qurate Retail renamed QVC Group, Feb 2025 — explicit pivot to "live social shopping company" (WIN growth strategy).
- April 2025: launched first 24/7 live shopping channel on TikTok in US (@QVC).
- April 2026: entered Restructuring Support Agreement; filed prepackaged Chapter 11 (S.D. Texas) to cut debt while advancing WIN strategy. (No planned layoffs per company.)

## TikTok ban / ownership saga (event correlation)
- Jan 18-20, 2025: brief US TikTok outage; back on app stores Feb 2, 2025.
- Sep 25, 2025: Trump announced TikTok transaction; nonenforcement extended to Jan 22, 2026.
- Jan 22, 2026: "TikTok USDS Joint Venture LLC" established. ByteDance <20%; Oracle/Silver Lake/MGX 15% each (45%); other investors ~35%. Majority-American 7-member board; US data on Oracle cloud.

## Trust / "is it legit" theme
- Recurring consumer concerns: counterfeit/low-quality goods, non-delivery (small intl sellers), refund friction.
- Advice patterns: avoid unverified/low-rated sellers, unusually low prices, off-platform payment.
- Strong search presence of "is [platform] legit/safe" queries — trust is a gating factor for adoption.

## Live auctions (parallel trend)
- Real-time bid-based commerce, collectibles-led (cards, sneakers, coins, comics, Funko).
- Often paired with eBay Live, Whatnot, Fanatics Live, StockX Live — distinct audience/psychology vs fixed-price LS.
